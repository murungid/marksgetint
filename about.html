Our Story

About Marks Gate International

Marks Gate International is a duly registered and authorised company committed to advancing the quality of education in Uganda and the wider East African region. We partner with schools, government institutions, parents, and education professionals to deliver world-class educational resources, materials, and support services.

Founded on the principles of excellence, accessibility, and community empowerment, we strive to bridge gaps in education by providing affordable, curriculum-aligned products and services that make a measurable difference in learning outcomes.

From curriculum development and examination papers to career guidance and classroom design — we are a one-stop solution for educational institutions and professionals across the region.
